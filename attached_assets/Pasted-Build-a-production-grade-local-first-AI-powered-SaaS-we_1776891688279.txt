Build a **production-grade, local-first, AI-powered SaaS web application** named **Procurement Intelligence Platform** for Indian businesses to discover, analyze, prepare, manage, and optimize government/private tenders.

This platform is a **Pre-Bid Intelligence + Bid Preparation + Tender Workflow + Procurement Intelligence OS**.

⚠ IMPORTANT:
The platform must NOT auto-submit bids on external portals like Government e-Marketplace, Central Public Procurement Portal, or any state/private portals.

Final submission remains manual on external portals.

The system must provide:

* Tender discovery
* Tender analysis
* Eligibility matching
* Proposal generation
* BOQ pricing intelligence
* Team workflow
* Post-bid tracking
* AI recommendations
* Go / No-Go decision support
* Knowledge base reuse
* Analytics & optimization

No placeholders.
No pseudo code.
Generate complete working production-grade code.
